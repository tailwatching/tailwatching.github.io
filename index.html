How does the Tail Watching model work?


Tail Watching is a long-only, trend following model (i.e. Buy positions in trending assets).

Traditional portfolio strategies follow the idea that asset prices follow a normal distribution. However, the existence of fat tails suggests that asset returns are not normally distributed, but skewed.

The fat tails indicate that there is a probability (often larger than anticipated), that prices will move beyond 3 standard deviations. The Tail Watching model capitalizes on these tails in trending liquid markets to generate outsized returns.

Investment philosophy of Tail Watching:

Don't predict, just ride the trend for as long as you can
Cut your losers short, let your winners run
Diversify by asset class, industry & geography

What is the Tail Watching dashboard?

The Tail Watching dashboard provides live alerts for you to build a long-only, trend following portfolio. Each asset in the dashboard has been put through the Tail Watching model to estimate its profitability over time. An average of 10-20 years of live data was used for each asset, creating live signals backed by decades of positive expectancy. The dashboard is a culmination of thousands of research & modelling hours packed into a table of actionable insights for you.

The dashboard can serve the following purposes:

  • Tail watching: Spot fat tails that are often missed by retail investors, and capitalize on potential upside
  • Trend filtering: Identify the latest trending stocks across multiple exchanges in a snapshot
  • Market heatmap: Feel the pulse of the market across various asset classes, geographies and industries
  • Entry signals: Use the signals as standalone or complimentary data points to build your portfolio
